DEAN OF FACTULY OF COMPUTERS AND DATA SCIENCE

DR.Magda Madbouly


Faculty of Computers and Data science , Alexandria University is one of the leading institutions in Egypt in the field of Information Technology and Computer Science. The faculty aims to prepare highly qualified graduates in areas such as programming, artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and database management, offering a balanced education that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills. It provides a variety of academic programs at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels, with a strong focus on scientific research and keeping pace with global advancements in computing. The faculty also encourages participation in programming competitions, and organizes workshops and training sessions in collaboration with major tech companies to equip students for both local and international job markets.

Participation in Presidential Elections 2024

The Faculty of Computers and Data Science, Alexandria University, took part in the 2024 presidential elections under the slogan “Take part, stay positive”. The Dean, staff members, and students participated to highlight the importance of youth awareness and national responsibility.

Assessment Center Visit – Exam Quality Review

A university evaluation team visited the faculty to monitor first-term exams, ensure smooth procedures, and verify the availability of required tools and controls.

FCDS Graduation Ceremony (2022/23 & 2023/24)

The faculty celebrated two cohorts’ graduation, highlighting its role in AI, smart systems, and national development, with thanks to staff, families, and students. Source: Your provided screenshots from the official faculty Facebook posts.

“You’re Stronger Than Drugs” Awareness Campaign

Under the Dean’s attendance, the faculty joined a national anti-addiction campaign, sharing the 16023 hotline and assuring confidential, free support.

Evaluation Center Visit – Exam Monitoring

A delegation from the Measurement and Evaluation Center at Alexandria University visited the faculty to monitor exams and ensure quality standards. The visit included the Dean and faculty staff, focusing on fairness, integrity, and smooth exam procedures.

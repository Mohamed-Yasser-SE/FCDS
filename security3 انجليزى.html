Cybersecurity Program

Cybersecurity Program Objectives:

Analyze, design, and build secure IT systems.
Providing the necessary support, management, and maintenance to ensure the effective and efficient performance of IT and security systems.
Identify, analyze, and mitigate threats to IT systems or networks.
Investigation of cyber incidents/IT crimes, systems and networks, and digital evidence.
Collect and analyze cybersecurity information that may be used to improve the protection of systems and networks.
Analyze, review, and evaluate available cybersecurity information to determine its usefulness for investigations.
Providing leadership, management, and guidance to individuals and organizations to act effectively and consciously towards cybersecurity.
